Project Overview

I led a customer experience analytics project that automated the collection and analysis of Google Maps reviews for Tan Son Nhat Airport. The goal was to help management understand passenger sentiment, identify service pain points, and prioritise operational improvements.

Role & Scope

  • Project Leader
  • Designed the ETL pipeline from scraping to reporting
  • Defined the sentiment and keyword extraction workflow
  • Translated findings into an executive dashboard

Key Contributions

  • Built an ETL process to scrape and structure more than 8,300 Google Maps reviews into MongoDB.
  • Fine-tuned the multilingual XLM-RoBERTa model to classify sentiment into positive, neutral, and negative categories with 82% accuracy.
  • Automatically extracted keywords to separate service issues into three dimensions: process, staff, and facilities.
  • Designed a dashboard with satisfaction KPI tracking, drill-down access to original comments, and a priority matrix for action planning.
