Vietjet Enhancement – Smart Chatbot & Delay Voucher

A business analysis project to modernise Vietjet Air customer support with multilingual chatbot assistance and automated delay compensation.

UML Figma BPMN DFD ERD
RoleProject Manager / BA
ScopeProcess redesign
FocusChatbot + voucher
ToolsUML · Figma · RAD

Project Overview

I analysed Vietjet's customer service workflow and proposed a digital solution that reduces manual handling for flight-delay compensation while improving self-service support through an AI chatbot.

Role & Scope

  • Project Manager / Business Analyst
  • Mapped the current process and identified operational bottlenecks
  • Defined functional requirements for chatbot support and voucher issuance
  • Produced UX mockups and system logic flows in Figma

Key Contributions

  • Modelled the solution using BPMN, DFD, ERD, and Use Case diagrams.
  • Proposed an AI-powered multilingual chatbot for common support scenarios.
  • Designed an automated voucher workflow to improve compensation speed and consistency.
  • Ensured the proposed solution aligned with business goals and customer experience expectations.
